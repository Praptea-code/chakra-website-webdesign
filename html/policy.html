Shipping & Order FAQs

What happens once I place my order?

You will get a confirmation mail with your order details. Post which our team will pack and ship your order. Once your order is shipped, you will get the Shipping details on mail with the tracking number. You will receive the invoice along with the order shipment.

I did not get an order confirmation and the amount got deducted. What to do?

The amount will get auto reversed to your bank in minimum 5-7 working days. In case it has not been reversed please reach out to: chakra00@gmail.com or call us on +01-550 467

I got a damaged watch. I want to return it. What to do?

We are committed to quality and in case you've received a defective watch. Write to us at chakra00@gmail.com or call us at +977 9879843728 and inform about the order ID. We will arrange for a pickup and once we have picked up the watch, between 3-4 working days, we will refund 100% of your payment or try to provide you with a replacement of the same watch, basis your requirement.
