Our Webpage

Reference Webpage

The home page of Rolex website starts with a video which made the homepage really attractive so it was concluded that the homepage of Chakra should also start with a video. The nav bar of Rolex was not really as per our liking as it didnot have easy access to other pages which in our opinion decreases the user friendliness. So, we decided to put easy access to every other page in our nav bar promoting a user friendly website. While scrolling we found that Rolex's navbar didnot have sticky property but as we kept scrolling and came across the next window the nav bar of Rolex's website changed and that particular nav bar after the first window was sticky.Therefore, after taking things in consideration we decided to give sticky property to navbar and also used javascript to remove the logo from the next window as it took alot of space.

Titan features a section highlighting their best selling products which makes it easier for users to know about popular items.
